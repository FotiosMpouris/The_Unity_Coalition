About The League

A New Infrastructure for Reform

The American Reformers League is a groundbreaking infrastructure connecting reform-minded Americans across traditional divides. We bring together populist, independent, and reform-oriented voices to address our nation's most critical challenges through collaborative action.

The Culture That Animates Us

We are guided by core values that shape all our work: Transparency, Focus, Humility, Dignity, and Responsiveness. These principles enable us to create a foundation of trust and elevate public discourse.

A Framework For Unity

About the Founder

Patrick Clerkin’s path to establishing the American Reformers League emerged from a unique combination of technical expertise and civic engagement. His journey from mechanical engineer to community organizer has been marked by consistent efforts to bridge divides and foster collaboration across the country.
