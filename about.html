About Unity Coalition

The Unity Coalition stands as a groundbreaking infrastructure connecting reform-minded Americans across traditional divides. Operating as both a cultural movement and a structured platform, we bring together populist, independent, and reform-oriented voices to address our nation's most critical challenges.

Through our innovative digital hub, we create dedicated spaces where leaders, organizations, and citizens can collaborate beyond conventional silos. Our approach is guided by core values of transparency, focus, humility, dignity, and responsiveness, enabling us to serve as the vital bridge between different political perspectives.

The Culture That Animates Us

At the Unity Coalition, we're guided by core values that shape all our work:

  • Transparency: We lead with positive values before policies, creating a foundation of trust.
  • Focus: We emphasize reform and bridging divides rather than overturning and dividing.
  • Humility: We appreciate complexity and focus on managing and balancing issues rather than oversimplifying them.
  • Dignity: We promote civic revival with basic decorum, elevating public discourse.
  • Responsiveness: We function as a support group, think tank, and action network, adapting to evolving needs.

These principles enable us to extract and amplify the virtuous aspects of both populist and globalist perspectives while minimizing their more destructive tendencies.

Understanding Our Historical Moment

America stands at a pivotal moment in its 250-year journey. Our nation has evolved through distinct eras—from Federalization (1788-1860) under Washington to Lincoln's Industrialization era (1860-1932), Roosevelt's Globalization period (1932-2016), and now into the Informationalization age that began taking shape in 2016.

Each transition has brought profound realignments in our political landscape, with parties transforming to address the challenges of their time. Today, we stand at another critical juncture where populist and globalist forces must find common ground for America to thrive. The Unity Coalition serves as the vital hinge connecting these perspectives, creating space for productive dialogue and collaborative action.

About the Founder

Patrick Clerkin's path to establishing the Unity Coalition emerged from a unique combination of technical expertise and civic engagement. With a background in mechanical engineering and experience in the semiconductor industry, Patrick brings an analytical perspective to complex social challenges.

His journey toward founding the Unity Coalition was shaped by extensive travels throughout America's heartland, where he drove over 35,000 miles to experience the country firsthand—not through the lens of pundits, but through direct engagement with communities between the coasts and off the interstates.

Patrick's prior civic experiences include running for City Council on a moderate, independent platform and developing a community network to address local civic erosion. Throughout his endeavors, he has consistently found himself "intermediating and reconciling between groups" rather than fitting squarely within any single faction—a quality that uniquely positions him to lead an organization dedicated to bridging America's divides.

The Path Forward: Our 30-Year Vision

The Unity Coalition operates with both immediate action and long-term vision. Our strategic timeline reflects our commitment to steady, deliberate progress:

  • 2025: Focus on agenda refinement, recruitment, cultural development, and infrastructure building
  • 2026: Run our first slate of independent candidates in strategically selected districts
  • 2027-2028: Collaborate with populist Republicans to influence party reform
  • 2029-2032: Work with globalist Democrats to advance international cooperation frameworks
  • 2033-2055: Foster unified independents' collaboration with transformed Republican and Democratic parties

This methodical approach ensures that our coalition builds sustainable momentum while fostering the gradual evolution of America's political landscape toward greater cooperation, effectiveness, and representation.
