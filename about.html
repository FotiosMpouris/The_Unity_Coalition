About The League

A New Infrastructure for Reform

The American Reformers League is a groundbreaking infrastructure connecting reform-minded Americans across traditional divides. Operating as both a cultural movement and a structured platform, we bring together populist, independent, and reform-oriented voices to address our nation's most critical challenges.

Through our innovative digital hub, we create dedicated spaces where leaders, organizations, and citizens can collaborate beyond conventional silos, guided by core values of transparency, focus, and dignity.

The Culture That Animates Us

At the American Reformers League, we're guided by core values that shape all our work:

  • Transparency: Leading with positive values before policies to build a foundation of trust.
  • Focus: Emphasizing reform and bridging divides rather than overturning and dividing.
  • Humility: Appreciating complexity and focusing on managing issues, not oversimplifying them.
  • Dignity: Promoting civic revival with basic decorum to elevate public discourse.
  • Responsiveness: Functioning as a support group, think tank, and action network.

A Framework For Unity

About the Founder

Patrick Clerkin’s path to establishing the American Reformers League emerged from a unique combination of technical expertise and civic engagement. His journey from mechanical engineer to community organizer has been marked by consistent efforts to bridge divides and foster collaboration.

Through extensive cross-country travels, local political involvement, and leadership in grassroots initiatives, Patrick developed a deep understanding of America’s challenges, shaping his vision for a platform where citizens can work together beyond partisan boundaries.
