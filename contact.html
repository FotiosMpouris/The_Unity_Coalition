Contribute

As a grassroots initiative built on principles of transparency and collective action, we rely on crowd-sourced support to maintain our independence and build the necessary infrastructure for lasting reform. Every contribution directly funds our core operations, including development of our communication platform, legal framework, and coordination across our network of reform initiatives.

Why Your Support Matters

The Unity Coalition represents a different approach to political reform—one focused on building bridges rather than walls, and finding common ground rather than amplifying divisions. Your contribution helps create a sustainable infrastructure for this bridge-building work in several key ways:

  • Establishing Our Core Team: Your support helps maintain a dedicated team focused on coordinating reform efforts across the country.
  • Building the Communication Hub: Contributions fund the development of our digital platform that connects reform-minded Americans across traditional divides.
  • Creating Educational Resources: Your support enables us to develop and distribute materials that help Americans understand our current challenges in historical context.
  • Organizing Connection Events: Funding supports summits and gatherings that bring together diverse stakeholders to coordinate reform efforts.

We believe in complete transparency in our operations and funding, which is why we've chosen to use Give Send Go as our donation platform, where you can find detailed information about our funding goals.

Your support, at any level, helps ensure that this critical work of civic renewal remains independent, effective, and true to its mission of unifying reform efforts across America.

Join Our Monthly Giving Program

Regular monthly contributions provide the sustainable funding needed for long-term planning and impact. By joining our monthly giving program, you become a foundational supporter of our work to create a more unified, effective political ecosystem.

Monthly supporters receive special briefings on our progress, early access to events and resources, and direct communication channels with our team.

To become a monthly contributor, simply select the "Make this a monthly donation" option when contributing through GiveSendGo.
