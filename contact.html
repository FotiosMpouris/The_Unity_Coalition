Connect With The League

Your involvement is a direct investment in America's civic revival. If you're interested in learning more, sharing resources, or joining our efforts, please reach out. Our primary point of contact is through email, allowing us to efficiently direct your inquiry to the right team member.

Contribute to the Mission

As a grassroots initiative, we rely on crowd-sourced support to maintain our independence. Every contribution directly funds our core operations, platform development, legal framework, and coordination across our network of reform initiatives.

Why Your Support Matters

  • Core Team: Maintain a dedicated team to coordinate reform efforts.
  • Communication Hub: Fund our digital platform connecting reform-minded Americans.
  • Connection Events: Support summits that bring together diverse stakeholders.

Join Our Monthly Giving Program

Regular contributions provide sustainable funding for long-term impact. Monthly supporters receive special briefings, early access to events, and direct communication with our team.

To join, simply select the "Make this a monthly donation" option on GiveSendGo.
