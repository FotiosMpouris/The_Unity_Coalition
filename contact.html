Contribute to the Mission

As a grassroots initiative, we rely on crowd-sourced support to maintain our independence. Every contribution directly funds our core operations, including platform development, our legal framework, and coordination across our network of reform initiatives.

Why Your Support Matters

  • Establishing Our Core Team: Your support helps maintain a dedicated team focused on coordinating reform efforts.
  • Building the Communication Hub: Contributions fund the development of our digital platform that connects reform-minded Americans.
  • Organizing Connection Events: Funding supports summits and gatherings that bring together diverse stakeholders.

Join Our Monthly Giving Program

Regular contributions provide sustainable funding for long-term impact. Monthly supporters receive special briefings, early access to events, and direct communication with our team.

To become a monthly contributor, simply select the "Make this a monthly donation" option when contributing through GiveSendGo.
