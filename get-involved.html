Get Involved

Why Get Involved?

Joining the American Reformers League means becoming part of a structured, purpose-driven network that goes beyond traditional activism. We need your help to combine wisdom and vision with strategy and action. Together, we can build a more stable and dignified order fit for our time.

What We're Doing: Our Current Initiatives

Building Our Core Team

Assembling a diverse team of dedicated individuals with complementary skills to guide and implement our mission.

Selecting Districts & Candidates

Identifying strategic districts where independent voices can have maximum impact and vetting candidates who align with our values.

Organizing Summit Events

Convening gatherings that bring together diverse stakeholders to coordinate reform efforts, share resources, and develop strategy.

What We Need From You: Three Ways to Help

1. Connections & Networking

Your introductions to influential moderates, independents, Republicans, and Democrats can significantly advance our work.

2. Funding & Partnerships

Direct contributions or connections with funding partners enable our core operations, communication, and organizing efforts.

3. Media & Promotion

Helping us reach wider audiences through media connections or content creation amplifies our message and expands our network.
