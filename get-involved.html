GET INVOLVED

Getting involved with the Unity Coalition means joining a structured, purpose-driven network that goes beyond traditional political activism. Whether you can offer your time, expertise, resources, or financial support, there are multiple ways to make a meaningful impact within our coalition.

We need your help to combine wisdom and vision with strategy and action. Together, we can build a more stable and dignified order fit for our time.

What We're Doing: Our Current Initiatives

Building Our Core Team

We're assembling a diverse team of dedicated individuals with complementary skills and perspectives to guide and implement our mission. This includes organizers, communicators, policy experts, technologists, and community builders.

Selecting Districts and Candidates

We're identifying strategic districts where independent voices can have maximum impact, and vetting potential candidates who align with our values of transparency, focus, humility, dignity, and responsiveness.

Organizing Summit Events

We're planning and convening gatherings that bring together diverse stakeholders to coordinate reform efforts, share resources, develop strategy, and strengthen our network of relationships.

What We Need From You: Three Ways to Help

1. Connections and Networking

We need your help connecting with influential moderate independents, republicans, and democrats who may be interested in our coalition-building approach. If you have relationships with potential allies, your introductions can significantly advance our work.

2. Funding and Resource Partnerships

Building an effective infrastructure for nationwide reform requires sustainable resources. Whether through direct contributions or connecting us with potential funding partners, your support enables our core operations, communication platforms, and organizing efforts.

3. Media and Promotional Opportunities

Helping us reach wider audiences through media connections, content creation, or promotional partnerships significantly amplifies our message and expands our network. If you have skills or connections in media, communication, or content production, these can be invaluable contributions.

Join Our Team

We need your help to combine wisdom and vision with strategy and action. The challenges facing America today require both thoughtful analysis and practical implementation—your involvement can bridge this gap.

By joining our team of motivated, focused, and capable independents—both party-affiliated and unaffiliated—you become part of a movement dedicated to bringing forth a more stable and dignified order fit for our time.

Whether you can commit a few hours a month or want to take on a leadership role, your contribution matters in our collective effort to reunite America.
