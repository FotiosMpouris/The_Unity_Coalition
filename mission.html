Our Mission

Union and Independence

The American Reformers League is dedicated to reuniting America's fragmented reform movements into a cohesive, action-oriented force. By creating robust cultural and infrastructural frameworks, we enable scattered reform efforts to consolidate their goals, share resources, and work together effectively across traditional boundaries.

Our Four-Part Mission Strategy

1. Mobilize

Bring together independent networks into an adaptive, influential coalition structure.

2. Platform

Develop and advocate for reform agendas that create concrete pathways for change.

3. Realign

Forge mutual alliances within evolving political parties, transcending partisan divides.

4. Campaign

Support key players to build balanced campaigns that blend autonomy with coordination.

Why Independents Must Unify

Like a flock of starlings demonstrating the power of adaptive collective movement, we prove that independents can unify and interdepend without sacrificing their autonomy. We serve four essential functions:

  • Guidance: Shaping new alignments and setting reform agendas.
  • Bridge Building: Connecting emerging party functions for collaboration.
  • Facilitation: Creating environments for moderation and negotiation.
  • Support: Providing a coherent third space for reform-minded Americans.

It Matters That We're United.
