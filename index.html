America at a Crossroads

Standing at a pivotal moment in our nation's 250-year journey, the Unity Coalition recognizes the historical patterns that have shaped American political life. From Federalization (1788-1860) through Industrialization (1860-1932) and Globalization (1932-2016), we now find ourselves in an era of Informationalization that demands new approaches to governance and civic engagement.

This fourth major transition in American history presents both unprecedented challenges and extraordinary opportunities to heal our divisions and forge a more perfect union. The Unity Coalition stands ready to be the transformative force our times demand.

Join our team of motivated, focused and capable independents – party-affiliated and unaffiliated – to bring forth a more stable and dignified order fit for our time

Uniting America's Reform Movement

In an era of unprecedented division and civic decline, the Unity Coalition emerges as a pioneering framework for reuniting Americans across traditional political and social boundaries.

The Unity Coalition serves as the essential bridge connecting the constructive elements of both populist and globalist perspectives. By transcending traditional partisan divides, we create a unified framework where independents can collaborate with reform-minded citizens across the political spectrum to address our nation's most pressing challenges.

Against the emboldened force of radical voices, polarized parties and captured institutions, honorable and capable Americans of all stripes have become scattered, drowned out and demoralized.

Our mission is not merely to mitigate polarization, but to facilitate a historic realignment where the virtuous aspects of populism and globalism can coexist and complement each other. Like the flock of starlings forming beautiful, adaptive patterns in the sky, we demonstrate that independents can simultaneously maintain autonomy while coordinating effectively for the common good.
